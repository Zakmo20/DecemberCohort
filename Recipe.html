Recipe of a Pancake

Shopping List

  • Plain flour
  • Large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • sunflower or vegetable oil

Cooking List

  1. Put 100g plain flour
  2. 2 large eggs
  3. 300ml milk
  4. 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil
  5. a pinch of salt into a bowl
